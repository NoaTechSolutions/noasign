●Legal protection

Every signature carries
full legal weight.

A signature on paper can be disputed. A signature through NTSsign cannot. Every document is backed by a complete, verifiable chain of evidence that protects you if a dispute ever arises.

✦
ESIGN Act compliant in the US
NTSsign uses BoldSign as its signing engine — the same technology trusted by enterprises. Every signature meets the full legal requirements of the ESIGN Act.
✦
Certificate of completion in every PDF
Timestamp, IP address, device, and action log — attached directly to every signed document. If anyone ever disputes a signature, you have proof.
✦
Tamper-evident — any change is detectable
Once signed, the document is sealed. Any modification after signing is cryptographically detectable. What was agreed is what stands.
