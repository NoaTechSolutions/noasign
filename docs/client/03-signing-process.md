# The Signing Experience (Client / Recipient)

This page describes what your client experiences when they receive a document for signature through NTSsign.

No account, app, or software download is required for the recipient.

---

## What the client receives

When you send a document, your client receives an email with:

- A clear subject line identifying the document
- A brief message explaining what to sign
- A secure **"Review & Sign"** button

The link is unique to that document and that recipient. It cannot be reused or shared to sign on behalf of someone else.

---

## Opening the document

The client clicks the link and is taken directly to the signing interface — powered by BoldSign, our e-signature provider. The interface is:

- Fully web-based — works on any browser or mobile device
- No registration required
- Guided — the interface highlights each field that needs to be filled or signed

---

## Signing

The client:

1. Reviews the document (scrolls through all pages)
2. Fills in any required fields (name, date, initials, etc.)
3. Draws or types their electronic signature
4. Clicks **Finish** to complete the signing

---

## After signing

Once the client clicks Finish:

- They see a **confirmation page** on NTSsign (signature-complete page)
- A copy of the signed document is sent to their email automatically
- You receive a notification and the document status updates to **Signed** → **Completed** on your dashboard

---

## What happens if the client doesn't sign?

If the client doesn't act on the signing link within a set timeframe, the document remains in **Sent** or **Viewed** status. You can:

- Follow up with the client directly
- Cancel the document and resend with an updated expiry date (planned feature: automatic reminders)

---

## Declining to sign

The client has the option to **Decline** the document if they do not agree to its terms. If they decline, the document status updates and you are notified so you can follow up.

---

## Security and legal validity

Every signature collected through NTSsign:

- Is backed by a complete **audit trail** (IP address, timestamp, device info)
- Complies with the **ESIGN Act** (US) for electronic signatures
- Generates a tamper-evident signed PDF
- Includes a certificate of completion

The signed PDF and its audit certificate serve as legal proof of agreement.

---

## Questions your clients might ask

**"Is this safe?"**
Yes. The signing link is unique to them and uses HTTPS. Their signature is recorded with a full audit trail.

**"Do I need to create an account?"**
No. Signing requires only the link sent to their email.

**"Can I sign on my phone?"**
Yes. The signing interface works on any modern browser — desktop, tablet, or phone.

**"Will I get a copy?"**
Yes. A copy of the signed document is automatically emailed to them after completion.
