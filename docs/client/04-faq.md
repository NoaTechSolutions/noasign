# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Account

**How do I get an account?**

Click **Request access** on the login page at [https://app.ntssign.com](https://app.ntssign.com). A team member will review your request and send you login credentials.

**How do I reset my password?**

Click **Forgot password** on the login page. Enter your email and you'll receive a reset link.

**Can I have multiple users on my account?**

Yes, depending on your plan. Accounts on the **Launch** plan and above can have multiple team members. Contact us to add users, or manage them from the **Members** section if you have a Master or Admin role.

---

## Documents

**Can I save a document without sending it?**

Yes. Click **Save draft** and the document is saved as a Draft. You can return to it and edit or send it at any time.

**Can I edit a document after sending it?**

No. Once a document is sent, it cannot be edited. If you need to correct something, cancel the document and create a new one.

**My client didn't receive the email — what do I do?**

Ask them to check their spam or junk folder. If the email isn't there, verify the email address on the document is correct. If it's wrong, cancel the document and create a new one with the correct address.

**Can I resend the signing link?**

Not directly from the dashboard yet — this feature is on the roadmap. For now, you can cancel the document and send a new one, or contact us and we'll resend from the backend.

**What happens when a document expires?**

The signing link becomes invalid and the document status does not advance. Cancel the document and create a new one to restart the process.

## Signatures

**Is the electronic signature legally valid?**

Yes. NTSsign uses BoldSign as the signing provider. Signatures comply with the ESIGN Act (United States) and include a complete audit trail (IP, timestamp, device) that serves as legal proof of signing.

**Can the recipient sign on a phone?**

Yes. The signing interface works on any modern browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile.

**Does the recipient need an account?**

No. The recipient only needs the link from the email to sign. No registration required.

**Will my client get a copy of the signed document?**

Yes. Once the document is completed, a copy of the signed PDF is automatically sent to the recipient's email.

---

## Files and downloads

**Where can I download the signed PDF?**

Open the completed document on your dashboard and click **Download PDF**.

**Does the signed PDF include an audit trail?**

Yes. The downloaded PDF includes a certificate of completion with the signing timestamp, IP address, and audit record.

**How long are my documents kept?**

Document retention depends on your plan — from 90 days (Pay-per-contract) to 5 years (Scale) or unlimited (Enterprise). You will be notified before any documents are scheduled for deletion.

## Billing

**How am I charged?**

Billing depends on your plan. Monthly plans include a set number of documents per month. Documents beyond the limit are charged at an overage rate per document. Usage is visible in the **Billing** section of your dashboard.

**What counts as a document for billing?**

A document is counted once it reaches **Completed** status (all parties have signed). Drafts and cancelled documents do not count.

**What happens if I go over my plan's document limit?**

Additional documents are charged at the overage rate for your plan. You'll see this reflected in your monthly billing.
