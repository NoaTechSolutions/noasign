# Getting Started with NTSsign

Welcome to **NTSsign** — the document signing platform built to simplify how you create, send, and track business documents.

This guide walks you through your first login and the basic concepts you need to know.

---

## What NTSsign does

NTSsign lets you:

- Create business documents (contracts, agreements, invoices) from a guided form
- Send them to clients for electronic signature — no printing, no scanning
- Track when they were opened and signed, in real time
- Download the final signed PDF and keep it on record

---

## Logging in

Go to [https://app.ntssign.com](https://app.ntssign.com) and enter your email and password.

If you don't have an account yet, click **Request access** on the login page. A member of our team will create your account and send you login credentials.

> If you forgot your password, use the **Forgot password** link on the login page.

---

## Your dashboard

After login you land on the **Documents** page. This is your main workspace.

From here you can:

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Create a new document | Click **New document** (top right) |
| Find a document | Use the search bar or filters |
| Check a document's status | See the status badge on each row |
| Open a document | Click its row |

---

## Document statuses

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Created but not sent yet |
| **Sent** | Delivered to the recipient — waiting for them to open it |
| **Viewed** | Recipient opened the signing link |
| **Signed** | Recipient completed the signature |
| **Completed** | All parties have signed — final PDF is available |
| **Cancelled** | Document was cancelled before completion |

---

## Your profile

Click your name or avatar (top right) to access your **Profile**. Here you can update your company information, contact details, insurance data, and upload your company logo.

This information is used to pre-fill documents automatically — keep it accurate.

---

## Your team (Master and Admin users)

If your role is **Master** or **Admin**, you also have access to:

- **Members** — manage users on your workspace, create new team accounts, deactivate users
- **Billing** — view your plan and monthly usage
