# Creating and Sending a Document

This guide walks through the full process of creating a document and sending it to a client for signature.

---

## Step 1 — Click "New document"

From the Documents page, click the **New document** button in the top right corner.

A form wizard opens. The fields you see are customized for your account — they match the specific document type and template configured for you.

---

## Step 2 — Fill in the client details

The first section asks for your client's information:

- **Customer name** — full name of the person signing
- **Email** — this is where the signing invitation will be sent
- **Phone** and **Fax** (optional)
- **Address**, **City**, **State**, **Zip code**

> Make sure the email address is correct. The signing link will be delivered there.

---

## Step 3 — Fill in the project details

The second section covers the project or service:

- **Project address** — can be copied from the client address if it's the same
- **Start date** and **Estimated completion date**
- **Project description**
- **Internal notes** — these are visible only to you, not to the client

---

## Step 4 — Fill in pricing

The pricing section includes:

- **Contract price** — the total amount
- **Down payment** (optional)
- **Payment schedule** (optional)
- **Finance terms** (if applicable) — enable the Finance toggle to add finance charges and installment dates

All currency fields accept decimal values (e.g. `12500.00`).

---

## Step 5 — Other details

The last section has additional fields specific to your document type:

- **Salesman name** (if applicable)
- **State registration number**
- **Warranty years**

---

## Step 6 — Review and save as draft

After completing all required fields, click **Save draft**. The document is saved with status **Draft**.

You can come back to edit a draft at any time before sending.

---

## Step 7 — Send for signature

Open the draft document and click **Send for signature**.

NTSsign will:
1. Pre-fill your document template with the data you entered
2. Send a signing invitation to your client's email
3. Update the document status to **Sent**

Your client receives a professional email with a secure signing link. No account or app download is required on their end.

---

## Tracking the document

Once sent, the document status updates automatically:

- When your client opens the link → **Viewed**
- When they complete the signature → **Signed** → **Completed**

You'll be able to download the final signed PDF once the status reaches **Completed**.

---

## Downloading the signed PDF

Open the completed document and click **Download PDF**. The file includes the signed document and a full audit trail (who signed, when, and from which IP address).

---

## Cancelling a document

If you need to cancel a sent document (for example, the client's details changed), open the document and click **Cancel**. This voids the signing link. You can then create a new document with the corrected information.

---

## Tips

- Double-check the client's email before sending — the signing link goes directly there
- The **Internal notes** field is a good place to add context you don't want the client to see
- Use the **Finance** toggle only if your contract has finance terms — it will add those fields to the document
